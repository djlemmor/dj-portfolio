<ABOUT ME>

My name is DJ Lemmor Nuique and I'm a Junior Web Developer based in the Philippines. I have a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology from Consolatrix College of Toledo City and I've been working in the industry for over 5 years. During my career, I have gained experience using various web development tools, including Adobe Edge Animate, jQuery, Browser Developer Tools, Visual Studio Code, Git, Npm, Sass, Vue, Vite, and Vitest.

My professional goals are to enhance my technical skills, take on more leadership roles, and contribute positively to my organization.

My hobbies and interests outside of work include swimming, playing online games (DOTA 2, Cabal Online), and reading web novels, manga, and manhwa.
